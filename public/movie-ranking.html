My Favorite Films and Shows By Teddy

Top 3 of the Year

Spirited

A new twist on Charles Dickens' wonderful story, "A Christmas Carol", this is a musical with Ryan Reynolds and Will Ferral, that promises to keep you laughing.

Lucky

An animated story about a young woman stuggling with her lack of luck, that I am sure we all can understand. She accidently finds herself in the Land of Luck, thanks to a talking black cat.

Leverage & Leverage: Redemption

A group of master criminals form an accidental team that works to right wrongs. They take up where the law leaves off bringing justice where there was none.
